HARMONY IN THE HALLWAYS

Music flows through the halls of Vilnius Lyceum. We're all about making, playing, and sharing music. Now picture this: top-notch sound and video gear just steps away from where we learn!

RENOVATION UNDERWAY

Room 322 is currently undergoing transformation. We've initiated the process with a thorough cleanup and have taken detailed measurements to ensure a perfect fit for its new purpose.

CONTINUING WHAT WAS STARTED

Although Vilnius Lyceum initiated a podcast, its potential was curtailed after just two episodes due to inadequate space. Remarkably, those initial episodes garnered over 500 streams, underscoring the untapped enthusiasm within our community. Our aspiration is to build on such engaging initiatives, reaching out to the entire community and fostering an enduring platform for creative expression.

STREAMLINING STUDIO ACCESS

With its multifunctional capabilities, we anticipate the studio will attract numerous students during free periods and after school. To streamline access, we've created an electronic booking page, displaying studio availability and enabling easy reservations. This scalable system could extend to other school spaces like the main hall and reading room.

EXPENSES

Examples of other studios as well as consultations with technicians helped us decide on specific hardware and software. The calculated cost of the project is 7000 Eur.
